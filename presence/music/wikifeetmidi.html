A swing and a…? It's up to you. While some may find it to be a Disappointment, for others it might uh, none of the other track titles make any sense, whatever. At any rate, this one is a bit of an experiment. No matter how I may feel about it, I can never regret making it, because it's like hell yeah I made a MIDI remix album. I don't think I'm ever making an album like this again because I have no plans to return to Jummbox now that I know how to use FL Studio. So I'm glad it made the lucky leap through that small window of opportunity it had to exist.
