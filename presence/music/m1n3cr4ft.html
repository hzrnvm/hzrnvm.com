m1n3cr4ft

3 October 2024

After Very Much, I worked on a few errant mashups for an imagined deluxe or a "Very Much 2" (like that'd ever happen). One of them was a mashup with Sweden, and I fell in love with it. I released it as a single and called it m1n3cr4ft. Over the next few days, I made much more Minecraft mashups, and Raven asked me if I was making a Minecraft mashup album. I hadn't thought of that. But hell, why not? I decided to make it 9 tracks, just like Very Much, and match its length of Very Much (which I kind of failed at, but RYM shows the length of Very Much to be 19:28, and the length of m1n3cr4ft taken as a whole is 19:28, so). And I dropped it exactly one month after

  2. Dirty Potion 2
  3. I'm Like Bitch Where Is Your Mine
  4. Kill Minecraft
  5. Chirpy!
  6. Block Too Hot
  7. Entering the Raven Dimension
  8. Pick for Pick Lets Have a Dig Off - SuperSpeedRaven
  9. Minecraft (Epilogue)
