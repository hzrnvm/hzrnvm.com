Very Much 3

3 September 2025

Very Much 3… My greatest album, there is no doubt in my mind. I don't think I've ever cared about making a piece of art like I cared about this album. It took a lot out of me! It's so much more composed than any other Hzrn album is. Miles beyond even Gates. Gates was made by taking the cream of a very large crop. This was made carefully. Nothing was just thrown on.
So, if it's so great, what is it about? All great art is about something, Seinfeld nonwithstanding. That's a good question. I don't really know. I've been thinking about it for a few months. I think it's about Very Much. It's about how much can change in a year. It's about 2025. And it's most definitely about Hzrn.
