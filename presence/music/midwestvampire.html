In the rollout for Very Much 2, I tried recreating the circumstances of Very Much's drop. Since Whole Lotta Lesbianism was released on Youtube before the rest of the album, with cover art by my sister, naturally I would do the same thing here. Except I have a Bandcamp page now, so it would go there too. The song itself? It's fine. I kinda like the chorus but I can't really bring myself to care about a song that wasn't like, an idea. It just exists by necessity, as an direct rigid extension of the album premise.
