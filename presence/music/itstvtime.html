It's TV Time!

3 September 2025

Another EP that started from one track (OK Computer was one as well!), in this case the opener. I thought of the track at work, made it when I got home, and I immediately knew this was an EP idea. Honestly it was pretty simple to make, there was no real hitch or anything worth mentioning about the process of making it. I just put it together. Great little EP, I especially like listening to it in sequence with its sister, ODM, as an album-like experience.

  4. How I Saw Your Footage
