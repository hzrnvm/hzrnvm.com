I had been planning to get a Deltarune mashup tape volume 2 out, what with chapters 3 and 4 dropping that summer. I made some tracks. In the time I spent sitting on my ass, fellow Buttonmasher TDK Kanal dropped two (very good) Deltarune mashup albums. I'm glad I proposed the idea of a collab album, because I have no idea how I'd have organised my tracks otherwise. Here it was easy peasy: 4 tracks each, with a Raven intermission in between. Now, whose 4 tracks do you like better? Who wins? You decide!
