Buttonmashers:
The Hzrn Collection

8 August 2025

Jesus christ, man. This comp is a fucking experience. By definition it's a collection of every track I released as part of Buttonmashers. In listening it's more like 9 tracks slowly travelling a year forward in time, until you reach the present and you're hit with a barrage of summer 2025 Hzrn tracks. Here you'll see almost everything I made that summer that didn't end up on Gates or Hzllbound and also that didn't suck. I limited myself to 43 tracks on the concurrently-released Buttonmashers self-titled album (an even crazier experience), so there is quality control here. I actually do recommend listening to this from start to finish, and read the liner notes!! The liner notes are so important
