Baby

24 July 2025

Oh yeah, Baby... that's what I'm talking about. I'm talking about Baby, it's Baby I'm talking about. Baby is my favourite Hzrn mixtape by a long shot, and one of my favourite records ever. It's really something, you know? Whereas many of my records are just a collection of songs (good collections, but collections nonetheless), Baby is something. Baby is an analysis of Justin Bieber's Baby-- by the real meaning of analysis, taking apart to understand. I wanted you to see all angles of the song. Everything it is, everything it could be, and everything it keeps within it. Bieber defenders often cry that Baby haters don't get it, but I don't want to sit around complaining. I'm gonna make you get it.
