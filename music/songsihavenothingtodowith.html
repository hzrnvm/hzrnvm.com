Songs I Have Nothing To Do With

3 September 2025

A dump of every unreleased song I had that point. September 3rd was supposed to be the beginning of my "fake retirement", and I didn't want any weight on me when I started. I listened to this once. It was interesting. Depending on where you draw the line, most of this is demos. Also okay I sequenced it in reverse alphabetical order, and like… why?! Because I didn't want 2017 to be the opener? There's other solutions, agh! It gets better as it goes on. If you don't want to listen to the full thing (reasonable choice, neither do i) I recommend you check out The Worst. It's ironically maybe the best song here
