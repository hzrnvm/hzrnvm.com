413

3 March 2025

Ah, 413… Or, as the folks in the retirement home I live in call it, the big one. And big it is, 48 tracks and almost 2 hours of straight 413 action. This is just a compilation of m1n3cr4ft, und3rt4l3 + d3lt4run3, and hom3stuck with some bonus tracks, released on the halfyear anniversary of Very Much. I rarely listen to it in full, but I used to listen to the bonus tracks on their own pretty frequently. Jolly good stuff!

Also, it's worth mentioning here that 413 has an unofficial sequel, 414. It consists of every track released on September 3rd, 2025. Can you find them all? Yeah, I think you can. Go forth!
