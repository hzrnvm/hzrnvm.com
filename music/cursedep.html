Cursed EP

27 January 2025

Cursed was an awesome single. So naturally the Cursed EP is an awesome EP. Mostly. I'm not gonna tell you the one significant flaw I find in it, so as to avoid the risk of implanting it in your mind myself. But I'll just say that frequently when I sat down to work on music I said to myself, "after I'm done with these songs I'll fix Imagine Though". And then I never did. But the other three songs rock. Cursed is obviously Cursed, Dreams Where I Am Dying is funny, and I Don't Know How is damn sweet mash.
This cover art is not bad at all but I wish it was good instead.

  1. Cursed
  2. Dreams Where I Am Dying
  3. I Don't Know How
  4. Imagine Though...
