Very Much

3 September 2024

We begin at Very Much. This album came about when I got really sick one day, the day before my 18th birthday, and I couldn't stand up from my laptop at all, so I decided to just practice making mashups. I made 4 songs, and decided to add 5 songs I had lying around and make a record of it. Fun fact about this one! It was almost called VeryMuch or Verymuch. I changed it at the very last minute. It's a little rough around the edges, but I was really proud of it when it dropped. I'll always look back on it as the genesis.
