The Gates of Hzrn

12 June 2025

Ahhh, Gates. My pop album, as I like to refer to it when I talk to nobody ever. But if I were to use such a turn of phrase, it would be very justified! This may very well be my most broadly enjoyable album yet. It gives a friendly almost-exactly 40 minutes of mostly really straightforward and high-quality mashups. You won't find a wqg or a stuck2u or an anything_from_wikiFeet_MIDI here, no sirree. But I managed to keep things interesting, too, for the most part! Songs like DDT and Digital Digital and Outro 1 and Remain Based are genre-bending fun. Also shoutout to Gloves for their great work on the cover art!
