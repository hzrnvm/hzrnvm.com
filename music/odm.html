ODM

3 September 2025

I was really into Ol' Dirty Bastard for a moment around early August. He's very dope! I made track 1 of this, sent it to the Buttonmashers, Raven called it "ODM", and I knew I had to make an EP of it. Also I don't really have connections to the guy who designed this album cover anymore, I never really knew him. Just wanted to make that explicit! I don't want to remove his name from the credits because I'm a good person.
