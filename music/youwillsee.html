You Will See

15 November 2025

If my previous mixtape was me "just doing whatever", I have no idea what I'd call this. This is a true Hzrn release in its blood, though: only put together because I was enjoying tracks I had made in my file browser, and I wanted to listen to them as I do any music. The only tracks I made after I decided to make the mixtape are tracks 7 and 9, and track 2 was only half done at that point. I still haven't listened to it in full, I didn't really have that in mind when making it. Love love love some of the tracks here down though
This cover art is one of my favourite images I've ever made. There's so much about it that I love. It's so ridiculous, it almost makes the struggle I had to go through to be in a position to find it feel worth it.
