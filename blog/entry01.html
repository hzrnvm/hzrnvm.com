What Is Blog 2?

Blog entry 01, written on day 9474 (Decmember 8th, 2025)

Welcome to Blog 2! Why is it called Blog 2? Because it's the sequel to blog 1, of course!

Hmm, what's that? You say you don't know of anything called "blog 1"? Blog 1 is my term of choice to refer to the blog on my previous personal website. It ran from 8120 (March 25th, 2022) to 9378 (September 02nd, 2025), with exactly 100 blog posts. That's 1258 days (isn't millenium time handy?), beginning when I was 15 and ending when I'd just turned 19.

Now, that's all well and good as a diachronic documentation of my latter-teen years. But I'm beginning adulthood now! (I think?) And an adult without a blog is like a proper gentleman without a monocle. So, with this blog post, my metaphorical proper gentleman form dons its metaphorical monocle.

Of course, an adult's blog is not the same as a teen's blog. Where my teen blog was filled with many posts where I just rambled a stream-of-consciousness (and some where I just wrote like 1 line), Blog 2 will be made of real Posts. Each post will have its own designated page, and they'll be long and focused and about something. Get excited!!
